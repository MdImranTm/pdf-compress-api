LinguaPDF
Universal Document Translator

LinguaPDF lets you read, translate, and organize your documents in any language — all privately on your device. No account needed, no data sent to any server except during translation.

How to Use
  • 📂 Add Document — Tap "Add Document", select a PDF, Word or text file. Language is auto-detected.
  • 🌐 Choose language — Pick your target language from 250+ options.
  • 🔄 Translate — Tap "Translate" on any chapter, or "All" to translate the whole document.
  • 📖 Read & compare — Switch between Original and Translated tabs while reading.
  • ✏️ Edit — Tap "Edit" to correct any translation or original text.
  • 📥 Download — Export as PDF, Word (.rtf) or TXT from any document.
  • ✍️ Text Translate — Go to Text Translate tab, paste or type any text, pick language and translate instantly. Copy or Save the result as TXT, Word or PDF.
  • 🔍 Search — Use the search bar in the header to find documents by name.
  • ☑️ Multi-select — Long-press the ⋮ menu on any document and tap Select to choose multiple documents for bulk delete.
  • 🌙 Theme — Toggle Dark/Light mode from the ⋮ menu or header switch.

Privacy Policy
Effective: March 2026 · LinguaPDF v1.0

In simple terms: LinguaPDF does not collect, store, or share your personal information. Your documents and data stay entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

LinguaPDF does not require an account and does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying data to use the app in its current version.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All your documents, translations, reading progress, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never uploaded to any external server by LinguaPDF. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

3. Translation Service

When you request a translation, the text content of that chapter is securely transmitted over HTTPS to our translation service. LinguaPDF does not log, store, or process the content of your documents on any server. Only the text needed for translation is sent, and only when you explicitly request it.

4. Internet Usage

Internet access is used only when you actively request a translation. All other features — reading, editing, browsing your library — work fully offline. No background data is ever sent.

5. File Access

LinguaPDF accesses files only when you explicitly choose a file through the file picker. The app does not scan, read, or access any other files, folders, or media on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

LinguaPDF does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages, contains no age-restricted content, and has no social or communication features.

7. Future Services

LinguaPDF may introduce optional features in the future — such as user accounts, cloud sync, or personalization features. If and when such features are added, this policy will be updated accordingly, and you will always have the choice to opt in. Any future data collection will be clearly disclosed and require your consent.

8. Data Safety Summary

✅ Data shared with third partiesNone
✅ Encrypted in transitYes — HTTPS
✅ Account requiredNo
✅ Works offlineYes (except translation)
9. Global Privacy Compliance

LinguaPDF is designed to be privacy-friendly by default and aligns with major global privacy frameworks:

  • GDPR (EU/EEA) — No personal data processed; no cookies or consent banners required
  • CCPA (California) — No personal information sold, shared, or collected
  • COPPA (USA) — No data collected from children under 13
  • PDPA, LGPD, POPIA — No personal data collected or stored in any jurisdiction
10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
